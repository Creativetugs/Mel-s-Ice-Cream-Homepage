Bringing Community & Cones Together for Generations

For generations, Mel’s Ice Cream has been a staple of the Massachusetts community, bringing pure joy one cone at a time. What started back in 1960 has evolved into a highly trusted local favorite known for prompt, friendly service, premium frozen treats, and nostalgic event bookings.

Planning a birthday party, school assembly, local sports game, corporate dynamic day, wedding, or neighborhood gathering? We alleviate all event hosting stress by driving the fun right to your destination!
