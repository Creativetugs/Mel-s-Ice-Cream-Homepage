Our Heritage

A Massachusetts Tradition on Wheels

For generations, Mel’s Ice Cream has been part of the community, bringing joy one cone at a time. What started in 1960 has grown into a trusted local favorite known for friendly service, premium frozen treats, and unforgettable event experiences.

Whether you’re planning a birthday party, school event, company picnic, wedding, or neighborhood gathering — we make it easy to bring the ice cream truck right to you.
